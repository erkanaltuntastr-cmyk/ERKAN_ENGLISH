## Part 8 - Personal writing task

Write 8 original sentences about your real work. Each sentence must include one target noun phrase from this list:

- a delivery issue
- product quality

- a regular ordering system

After writing, check each sentence with this checklist:

1. Does the sentence have a clear subject?
2. Does the sentence have a full verb?
3. Does every singular countable noun have `a`, `an`, `the`, `my`, `this`, or another determiner?
4. Are plural nouns correctly plural?
5. Is the noun phrase natural in work English?
