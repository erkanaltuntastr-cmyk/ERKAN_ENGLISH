## Step 2 - Writing Sample

Write 150-250 words on one of these topics:

1. Why I want to improve my English now
2. A recent professional challenge I handled
3. How I use AI tools in my daily work

Assessment criteria:
- Grammar accuracy
- Vocabulary range
- Sentence structure
- Coherence and cohesion
- British English usage
- CEFR estimate

## Step 3 - Speaking Sample

Record or transcribe a 1-2 minute answer to one of these prompts:

1. Tell me about your current work and responsibilities.
2. Describe a project you are proud of.
3. Explain what kind of English you need most: meetings, writing, interviews, travel, or technical work.

Assessment criteria:
- Fluency
- Pronunciation clarity
- Grammar control
- Vocabulary range
- Self-correction
- CEFR estimate
