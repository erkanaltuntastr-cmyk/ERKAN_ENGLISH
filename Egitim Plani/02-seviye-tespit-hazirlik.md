# 02 - Seviye Tespit Hazirligi

Purpose: prepare a practical CEFR baseline for Erkan before starting the weekly learning plan.

Target style: British English first, with grammar and vocabulary references from the local book library.

## Diagnostic Structure

| Section | Time | Skill | Output |
| --- | ---: | --- | --- |
| Grammar diagnostic | 25 min | Grammar accuracy | Multiple choice + sentence correction |
| Vocabulary diagnostic | 15 min | General vocabulary and phrasal verbs | Meaning, usage, collocation |
| Reading diagnostic | 20 min | Reading comprehension | Short text + inference questions |
| Writing sample | 25 min | Writing | 150-250 words |
| Speaking sample | 10 min | Speaking | 1-2 minute answer or transcript |
| Self-assessment | 5 min | Needs and goals | Work, travel, AI, technical English |

Estimated total: 100 minutes. It can be split into two sessions.

## Source Strategy

Do not copy questions from the books. Use the books to choose grammar areas, difficulty, and common learner problems.

| Diagnostic Area | Main Reference | Secondary Reference |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Core grammar | BOOK-01 | BOOK-05, BOOK-06 |
| Weak grammar explanations | BOOK-08 | BOOK-05 |
| Advanced grammar check | BOOK-07 | BOOK-05 |
| Phrasal verbs | BOOK-10 | BOOK-09 |
| British English alignment | British Council, Cambridge, Oxford Learner's Dictionaries | BBC Learning English |

## Grammar Topics To Cover

The diagnostic should sample these areas without becoming too long:

- Present simple vs present continuous
- Past simple vs present perfect
- Future forms
- Articles: `a`, `an`, `the`, zero article
- Countable and uncountable nouns
- Modal verbs
- Conditionals
- Passive voice
- Relative clauses
- Gerunds and infinitives
- Prepositions
- Word order and sentence structure

## Vocabulary Topics To Cover

- Everyday work vocabulary
- Learning and technology vocabulary
- Common adjective-noun collocations
- Common verb-noun collocations
- Phrasal verbs used in work and daily life
- Formal vs informal wording

## Writing Prompts

Choose one:

1. Why I want to improve my English now
2. How I use AI tools in my work or learning
3. A professional problem I solved recently

Expected length: 150-250 words.

## Speaking Prompts

Choose one:

1. Tell me about your current work and responsibilities.
2. Describe a project you are proud of.
3. Explain the kind of English you need most and why.

Expected length: 1-2 minutes.
